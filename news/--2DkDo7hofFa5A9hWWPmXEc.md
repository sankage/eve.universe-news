at the beginning of the [Gallente-Caldari War](1ehjby0lOpdwMJf9CprPtV).
The [Caldari](7unGNsrMFwIWXMMbrM2jfy) used [single-man fighters](2zJdxBu7hlAbyrhkl5sLou) against them.
