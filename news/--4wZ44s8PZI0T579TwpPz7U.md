# Kasora Neko
Published on  at https://universe.eveonline.com/new-eden-news/kasora-neko

**Kasora Neko** is the Grand Admiral of the [Republic Fleet](2c4ZwKPnuW6mYkLJ2dGh94). At one point she discussed the Elder
Invasion and [Amarr](6BPFRy27fN4LnYlIyzvEwo) reprisal with an unknown captain.
She agreed that [capsuleers](15umOALoFBZxVS2oaggvJQ) should be put to
fight in lowsec space, where millions of undefended people lived.
